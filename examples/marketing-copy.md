# Example: Marketing Copy Workflow

## Task
Create campaign directions for a benchmark-aware Instagram or Threads post.

## Divergence prompt
Give me 7 clearly different campaign directions for this topic.
Do not write the final post yet.
For each direction provide:
- direction name
- core metaphor
- opening hook idea
- CTA style
- emotional tone
- benchmark-fit note
- risk

## Selection prompt
Compare the 7 directions using:
- distinctiveness
- audience fit
- benchmark compatibility
- ease of execution
- scalability into a campaign
Return the top 2 only.

## Convergence prompt
Refine only the #1 direction into:
- headline
- body copy
- CTA
- 5 hashtags
Do not blend in the other directions unless explicitly requested.

## Anti-fixation check
Before finalizing, answer:
- Did we explore enough truly different directions?
- Are we just polishing the first plausible idea?
- Did benchmark usage remain structural rather than imitative?
