# Failure Modes

This file defines the failure patterns that most often degrade AI-assisted creative work.

## 1. First-result anchoring
Symptoms:
- the first plausible direction gets refined immediately
- later directions feel weaker because they are judged against a polished first answer
- the discussion becomes wording-level too early

Counter:
- ban final deliverables in divergence stage
- require 5-9 directions first
- compare before refining

## 2. Fake divergence
Symptoms:
- directions use different labels but share one worldview
- all hooks are the same mechanism with cosmetic wording changes
- only tone changes, not concept logic

Counter:
- require worldview, persuasion mechanism, and risk fields
- reject sets where top directions can be merged without loss

## 3. Benchmark mimicry
Symptoms:
- output feels familiar in a traceable way
- the cadence, slogan shape, or CTA pattern resembles one competitor too closely
- the team says "it sounds like winners" as justification

Counter:
- extract structure only
- add competitor-recognition test
- score derivative risk explicitly

## 4. Premature convergence
Symptoms:
- scoring happens informally during ideation
- one direction is treated as default before comparison
- the group skips explicit ranking

Counter:
- force a separate selection stage
- keep only top 2 after scoring

## 5. Hybrid mush
Symptoms:
- final concept mixes unrelated directions
- the result becomes broad, safe, and diluted
- no single conceptual spine survives

Counter:
- converge on one winner only
- do not blend unless explicitly required

## 6. Benchmark-shaped originality
Symptoms:
- output is technically clean but feels category-native in a generic way
- strong performance logic, weak authorship
- it could belong to any competent team

Counter:
- increase novelty target discipline
- force remote analogies or category inversion

## 7. Over-intellectualized output
Symptoms:
- strategically impressive, executionally heavy
- long sentences, too much meta explanation
- not native to the platform rhythm

Counter:
- add execution-depth check
- compress after convergence, not before

## 8. Unsafe persuasion drift
Symptoms:
- overclaiming
- clickbait
- competitor imitation
- manipulative fear framing

Counter:
- define disallowed patterns before ideation
- add explicit safety note in final output
